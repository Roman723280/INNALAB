# Campaign Timeline: Finding the EU Lead Partner

An 8-week intensive outreach plan to secure a Lead Partner for the Interreg NEXT PL-UA grant.

---

## Week 1: Preparation & Setup
*   **Day 1-2:** Finalize English-language "Partner Pitch" PDF (based on `value_proposition.md`).
*   **Day 3:** Set up a dedicated "Partners" landing page section on `innalab.org.ua`.
*   **Day 4-5:** Verify contact details for the top 8 target institutions in `target_institutions.md`.

## Week 2: Direct Outreach Phase I
*   **Day 1:** Send "Initial Contact" emails to the top 4 academic targets (ASP Kraków, UMK Toruń, ASP Warsaw, NID).
*   **Day 3:** Launch first LinkedIn post (Pillar A: Partnership Call).
*   **Day 5:** Post partnership announcement on *Heritage Research Hub*.

## Week 3: PR & Community Engagement
*   **Day 1:** Submit project summary to E-RIHS news portal.
*   **Day 2:** Launch second LinkedIn post (Pillar B: Technical Authority).
*   **Day 3-4:** Follow-up calls/emails for Phase I direct outreach.
*   **Day 5:** Reach out to the remaining 4 targets from the list.

## Week 4: Follow-up & Deep-Dives
*   **Day 1:** Send "Value Deep-Dive" follow-up emails to non-responders.
*   **Day 2:** Schedule first introductory Zoom calls with interested leads.
*   **Day 4:** Launch third LinkedIn post (Pillar C: Mission & Impact).

## Week 5: Technical Briefings
*   **All Week:** Conduct technical briefings for interested institutions.
*   **Action:** Share MobiBOX technical specifications and draft budget templates.
*   **Goal:** Narrow down to 2-3 high-probability Lead Partner candidates.

## Week 6: Selection & Letter of Intent (LoI)
*   **Day 1-2:** Request formal expression of interest from top candidates.
*   **Day 3:** Select the primary Lead Partner.
*   **Day 4-5:** Draft and send the Letter of Intent (LoI) for signature.

## Week 7: Application Drafting (Joint)
*   **All Week:** Collaborative work on the Interreg application packages.
*   **Action:** Finalize Lead Partner staff hours and in-kind valuation.
*   **Action:** Detail the joint scientific seminar plan.

## Week 8: Final Review & Submission
*   **Day 1-3:** Administrative review of all project documentation.
*   **Day 4:** Final internal sign-off by INNALAB and the Lead Partner.
*   **Day 5:** Official submission of the Track A application to the Interreg Joint Secretariat.

---
**Key Milestones:**
*   **End of Week 2:** First potential lead identified.
*   **End of Week 4:** 50% of direct outreach targets engaged in dialogue.
*   **End of Week 6:** Letter of Intent signed.
