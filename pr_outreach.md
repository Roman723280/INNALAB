# PR and Community Outreach Plan: INNALAB-CURE

Identify and target specific heritage science platforms and journals to broadcast the project mission and find academic partners.

## 1. Targeted Platforms

### E-RIHS (European Research Infrastructure for Heritage Science)
*   **Why:** This is the primary EU network for heritage science labs.
*   **Action:** Submit a project summary to the E-RIHS newsletter and news portal. Contact the Polish node (E-RIHS.pl) specifically.
*   **Contact:** portal@e-rihs.eu / Coordination at Nicolaus Copernicus University in Toruń.

### Heritage Research Hub
*   **Why:** A platform specifically designed for cultural heritage research news and partnership searches.
*   **Action:** Post a "Partner Search" announcement in their dedicated funding/partnership section.
*   **Website:** [heritageresearch-hub.eu](https://www.heritageresearch-hub.eu/)

### IIC (International Institute for Conservation) - News
*   **Why:** Globally recognized body for conservation professionals.
*   **Action:** Draft a short "News from the field" article about the MobiBOX deployment for their *News in Conservation* digital magazine.
*   **Website:** [iiconservation.org](https://www.iiconservation.org/)

### ALIPH Foundation Blog/News
*   **Why:** Since ALIPH is a potential funder (Track B), visibility on their platform attracts other conflict-heritage specialized partners.
*   **Action:** Share mission updates and tag ALIPH on social media to get reshared on their official channels.

## 2. Project Announcement Draft (for Community Boards)

**Title:** Partner Search: Lead Partner for Mobile Heritage Science Lab (Interreg NEXT PL-UA)

**Summary:**
The INNALAB-CURE project, Ukraine's first mobile scientific-restoration lab, is seeking an EU-based (Polish) academic or research institution to serve as the **Lead Partner** for a €99,510 Interreg grant (Track A) and subsequent Horizon Europe CL2.2 tracks.

**Technical Scope:**
INNALAB-CURE utilizes a fully autonomous mobile unit (MobiBOX) equipped with Bruker XRF, ThermoFisher FTIR, Raman, and Digital X-ray for in situ diagnostic and emergency conservation of objects in conflict zones.

**Value for Partners:**
*   **Data:** Access to unique datasets from unexplored heritage objects.
*   **Academic:** Co-authorship in high-impact Heritage Science journals.
*   **Standardization:** Joint development of the 'Heritage First Responder' (HFR) certification.
*   **Financial:** 90% EU funding with strictly in-kind partner contribution (~€5,800).

**How to Apply:**
Interested institutions are invited to contact Project Lead Inna Dmytruk-Sorokhtei at **ina.dmytruk@gmail.com** for a technical briefing and project documentation.
