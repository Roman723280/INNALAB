# Target Institutions for INNALAB-CURE Partnership (Poland/EU)

The following institutions are identified as primary targets for finding a Lead Partner (Interreg NEXT PL-UA) or consortium partners (Horizon Europe).

## 1. Jan Matejko Academy of Fine Arts in Kraków (ASP Kraków)
*   **Faculty:** Faculty of Conservation and Restoration of Works of Art
*   **Relevance:** Leading Polish institution in restoration. Long-standing tradition of merging art and science.
*   **Contact:**
    *   Ms. Agnieszka Marecka (Faculty Coordinator): amarecka@asp.krakow.pl
    *   Website: https://www.asp.krakow.pl/faculty-of-conservation-and-restoration-of-works-of-art/

## 2. Nicolaus Copernicus University in Toruń (UMK Toruń)
*   **Faculty:** Faculty of Fine Arts / Department of Conservation and Restoration of Architecture and Sculpture
*   **Relevance:** Strong emphasis on material science and chemical analysis (FTIR, polymers) in conservation.
*   **Contact:**
    *   Dr hab. Piotr Niemcewicz (Head of Dept): piotr.niemcewicz@umk.pl
    *   Dr Marta Chylińska (Chemical analysis specialist): mch@umk.pl
    *   Website: https://kkrar.umk.pl/pages/Pracownicy/?langu=en

## 3. Academy of Fine Arts in Warsaw (ASP Warsaw)
*   **Faculty:** Faculty of Conservation and Restoration of Works of Art
*   **Relevance:** National-level authority. Hosts the Inter-Academy Institute of Conservation and Restoration of Works of Art (MIK).
*   **Contact:**
    *   Rectorate: rektorat@asp.waw.pl
    *   Erasmus+ Office: erasmus@asp.waw.pl
    *   Website: https://en.asp.waw.pl/

## 4. National Institute of Heritage (NID) / Polish Support Center for Culture in Ukraine
*   **Relevance:** Government-backed center specifically for cultural aid to Ukraine. Ideal for high-level coordination and endorsement of HFR (Heritage First Responder) credentials.
*   **Contact:**
    *   Website: https://ukraina.nid.pl/en/centrum-pomocy-dla-kultury-na-ukrainie-4/
    *   Email: dfe@kultura.gov.pl (Ministry of Culture, European Funds Dept)

## 5. POLONIKA National Institute
*   **Relevance:** Specialized state institution for preserving Polish cultural heritage abroad, with significant activities in Ukraine.
*   **Contact:**
    *   Website: https://polonika.pl/en/institute
    *   Focus: Renovation, conservation, and research collaboration.

## 6. Wawel Royal Castle - Conservation Workshops
*   **Relevance:** Practical application on UNESCO-level heritage. Chief Museum Conservator Dr. Aleksandra Hola is an expert in paintings on wood and has extensive EU network.
*   **Contact:**
    *   Dr. Aleksandra Hola
    *   Website: http://wawel.krakow.pl/en/conservation-of-works-of-art

## 7. University of Wrocław
*   **Faculty:** Faculty of Historical and Pedagogical Sciences
*   **Relevance:** Research-heavy institution. Could serve as a partner for Horizon Europe CL2.2 tracks.
*   **Contact:**
    *   Email: study.history@uwr.edu.pl
    *   Website: https://international.uni.wroc.pl/en/

## 8. European Research Infrastructure for Heritage Science (E-RIHS.pl)
*   **Relevance:** The Polish branch of the research infrastructure. They coordinate lab access and science-based heritage research across Poland.
*   **Contact:**
    *   Coordinated by the Nicolaus Copernicus University in Toruń.
    *   Website: http://e-rihs.pl/ (Polish)
