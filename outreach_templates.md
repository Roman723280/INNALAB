## 1. Initial Contact: The "Lead Partner" Invitation
**Subject:** Strategic Partnership Opportunity: Lead Partner for Interreg PL-UA (Heritage Science & Mobile Restoration)

**Dear [Recipient Name],**

I am writing to you on behalf of the INNALAB-CURE project, based in Lviv, Ukraine. We have developed Ukraine’s first mobile scientific-restoration laboratory, and we are currently seeking a Polish academic or research institution to serve as the **Lead Partner** for an upcoming Interreg NEXT PL-UA (Priority 4) grant application.

**Why this partnership?**
*   **Funding:** The project is 90% EU-funded (€99,510 for Track A).
*   **In-Kind Only:** The required partner contribution (~€5,800) is strictly in-kind; no financial investment is needed.
*   **Scientific Value:** You will gain exclusive access to unique XRF/FTIR datasets from heritage objects in conflict zones for research and joint publications.
*   **Prestige:** Your institution will lead the development of the "Heritage First Responder" (HFR) certification framework.

Our team has over 30 years of experience on UNESCO sites and recently attributed a previously unknown Georges de La Tour painting using our non-invasive diagnostic suite.

Would you be open to a 20-minute introductory call this week to discuss how [Institution Name] can benefit from leading this consortium?
